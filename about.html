About Hogan Home Improvement

"Hi! My name is Chris Hogan. I live in one of downtown South Lyon, Michigan's original city homes. My wife Kristina and I have spent the last 12+ years making improvements; both inside and out. I have many years of mechanical, electrical, drywall, plumbing, rough-framing, and finish-work construction experience. I have done it all; from installing single outlets to finishing entire basements. My two main focuses are quality work and customer satisfaction! I genuinely enjoy making my customer's happy with tangible improvements to their homes.

When not at work... Kristina and I tackle lots of extracurricular projects (both around the house and beyond). We make a really good team. We also enjoy camping trips with our two dogs. And, I love to ski with my nephew in winter."

-Chris Hogan
Owner
